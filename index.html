Pictorial Time Table

Lets consider time table for 2 upto 10 (means 2*10). Take a circle put 10 equidistance point, then we lebel them 0,1,2,...,9 and any number greater than 10 take mod of 10. Now draw line on the basis of 2 times table. Like :

  • 0*2 = 0 : draw line between point 0 to 0
  • 1*2 = 2 : draw line between point 1 to 2
  • 2*2 = 4 : draw line between point 2 to 4
  • 3*2 = 6 : draw line between point 3 to 6
  • 4*2 = 8 : draw line between point 4 to 8
  • 5*2 = 10 : mod 10 of 10 = 0 : draw line between point 5 to 0
  • 6*2 = 12 : mod 12 of 10 = 2 : draw line between point 6 to 2
  • 7*2 = 14 : mod 14 of 10 = 4 : draw line between point 7 to 4
  • 8*2 = 16 : mod 16 of 10 = 6 : draw line between point 8 to 6
  • 9*2 = 18 : mod 18 of 10 = 8 : draw line between point 9 to 8
  • 10*2 = 20 : mod 20 of 10 = 0 : draw line between point 0 to 0
    •

    Now if we increase upto by 1, we get pictorial 2 time table for 11,12,... so on. If we reach upto 100 or more. We get heart shape structure.

    If we increase times tables for 3,4,... so on. We get kidney shape structure for time table for 3. And get different structure for different time table. It is called heart of mathematics.
